Heavy rain caused waterlogging on roads.
Waterlogging slowed down traffic movement.
Slow traffic caused long delays.
Delays made people late to work.

Road construction reduced available lanes.
Fewer lanes caused traffic congestion.
Traffic congestion increased travel time.
Increased travel time caused frustration.

Signal failure caused traffic confusion.
Traffic confusion led to accidents.
Accidents blocked roads.
Blocked roads worsened traffic congestion.
